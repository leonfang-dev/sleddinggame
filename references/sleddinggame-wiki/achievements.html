Sledding Game — All 37 Achievements Guide

Sledding Game has 37 Steam achievements and 37 Xbox achievements worth 1,000 Gamerscore in total. This complete guide covers every achievement with unlock rates, step-by-step instructions, and tips — from the easiest (79.4% unlock rate) to the most legendary (0.1%).

📊 Complete Achievement List

🛷

Sledding Game

Easy 79.4% of players

Sled in the game.

How to unlock: Simply hop on any sled and slide downhill. This is the very first achievement almost every player gets within seconds of joining.
🚡

Taking Your Time

Easy 72.4% of players

Ride the chairlift.

How to unlock: Walk up to any chairlift station and interact with it. The main chairlift runs from the base lodge to the summit. Simply board and enjoy the ride up.
⭐

Fashion Star

Easy 65.3% of players

Equip an item not sold in the shop.

How to unlock: Unlock any secret collectible item (such as the Hard Hat from the cave, the Apple from the shooting range, or the Golden Tie from /secret command) and equip it from your inventory.
👕

Stylish

Easy 61.3% of players

Equip a clothing item.

How to unlock: Purchase any clothing item from the Lodge shop or the Gacha machine, then go to your character customization and equip it.
🗺️

Error 404

Medium 🔮 Secret 37.9% of players

Escape the map somehow.

How to unlock: Take the long left-side chairlift to its top station, then keep running left past the terminal. Continue past the map boundary. The Yeti will eventually kick you back — achievement unlocks the moment you cross the boundary.
🔄

Back Where I Started

Easy 26.8% of players

Return to spawn point.

How to unlock: Place a flag with the [1] key anywhere on the mountain, then press [2] to teleport back to it. Alternatively, use the in-game respawn option from the menu.
✨

My Sled is Cooler

Easy 23.2% of players

Customize your sled.

How to unlock: Visit the Sled Customization Table inside the Lodge. Apply any sticker, color, or trinket to your sled. Even a single cosmetic change counts.
🦣

Don't Wander Off

Easy 21.9% of players

Get kicked by the Yeti.

How to unlock: Deliberately leave the playable map area. The Yeti will appear and boot you back to the mountain. This often happens naturally when exploring the edges near the long left chairlift.
🎣

Fishing Game

Medium 21.7% of players

Catch a fish.

How to unlock: First, purchase a Fishing Rod from the Lodge shop for 10,000 coins. Then head to any fishing spot on the mountain (multiple exist near the summit). Cast your line and wait for a nibble, then interact to reel in.
❄️

Choosing Violence

Easy 21.5% of players

Hit someone with a snowball.

How to unlock: Equip the snowball (default toolbar item) and aim at another player. Works best in the base lodge area where players congregate. Lead your shot slightly since snowballs arc.
🍗

Chicken Dinner

Medium 16.0% of players

Win a mini-game.

How to unlock: Compete in any mini-game inside the Lodge (Darts, Curling, Cornhole, or the Hot Chocolate reaction game) and finish in first place. The Hot Chocolate reaction game is the easiest to win solo.
🪂

Soarin Flyin

Medium 15.7% of players

Get big air on a jump.

How to unlock: Build up maximum speed on the main slope, then hit the large wooden jump near mid-mountain. Hold forward and go full speed for maximum height. The jump near the Lodge gives consistent big air.
💫

This One Sparkles

Medium 15.1% of players

Equip a sparkle item.

How to unlock: Find or purchase any item tagged as a 'sparkle' cosmetic. These are typically obtained through the Gacha machine. Check your inventory for items with a ✨ sparkle icon.
⛄

Do you want to

Medium 13.7% of players

Build a snowman.

How to unlock: Find the snowman-building area in the outdoor section of the map. Interact with the snow pile to start rolling snowballs. Stack three snowball sizes (small, medium, large) to complete the snowman.
💨

Speed Demon

Medium 13.7% of players

Go over 100 m/s in a sled.

How to unlock: Use the fastest sled available (Snowmobile or Speed Sled recommended). Start from the very summit, hold full forward acceleration the entire way down without braking. The long straight run after the first chairlift station is ideal. Equip speed-boosting trinkets if available.
🎭

New Look

Easy 11.3% of players

Change your character.

How to unlock: Unlock any alternate character (see our Characters page) and switch to it via the Character Selection screen in the customization menu.
👫

Just the Two of Us

Easy 10.8% of players

Sled with another player.

How to unlock: Join any public server with at least one other player active. Simply sled down the mountain while another player is also sledding. No coordination required — just share the slope.
💥

Just a Bump

Easy 9.5% of players

Collide with another player.

How to unlock: Sled directly into another player on the mountain. The easiest method is to intentionally aim at a friend or other player near the bottom of a run where everyone converges.
🧍

Standing Game

Easy 🔮 Secret 7.2% of players

Stand still for a while.

How to unlock: Find a flat area, exit your sled, and simply don't move for about 30–60 seconds. The Lodge plateau is perfect. Go grab a snack in real life while your character idles.
🚀

Prepare for Takeoff

Medium 6.6% of players

Launch from the big jump.

How to unlock: Find the largest jump structure on the mountain — typically near mid-mountain on the main run. Build maximum speed coming in and go off the center of the ramp for maximum distance. This is a bigger version of Soarin Flyin.
🔥

Fireball

Medium 5.6% of players

Hit someone while going very fast.

How to unlock: Reach high speed (above ~80 m/s) and deliberately ram into another player. Have a friend stand at the bottom of the main slope and crash into them at full speed. Trinkets that increase speed help.
🎟️

Ticket Master

Grind 3.5% of players

Ride the chairlift many times.

How to unlock: Ride the chairlift repeatedly. The exact number is not disclosed, but community estimates suggest 15–25 rides. Each run up counts — make it your habit to always take the lift rather than respawning.
🪑

Sitting Game

Easy 🔮 Secret 1.4% of players

Sit down.

How to unlock: Type /sit in the in-game chat. Your character will sit on the ground. This is a hidden command that most players don't discover naturally, which explains the low unlock rate.
🚶

Walking Game

Medium 🔮 Secret 0.8% of players

Walk without a sled.

How to unlock: Dismount your sled by pressing the dismount key, then walk a meaningful distance on foot. Type /walk in chat if the dismount alone doesn't trigger it. Walk from the Lodge to the base of the first jump.
🏎️

Drive By

Medium 0.6% of players

Hit someone from a sled.

How to unlock: While on your sled at moderate-to-high speed, deliberately run into another player. This is different from Fireball — speed doesn't need to be extreme, but you must be actively sledding (not on foot) when contact is made.
🦈

The BIG one

Hard 0.4% of players

Catch the biggest fish.

How to unlock: Fish from the summit fishing spot (the highest elevation fishing point). This reportedly has the best chance of Great White Shark. Be patient — this is RNG-based. Fishing repeatedly from the summit gives the best odds. Expect 50–200 casts.
🪵

Bench Talk

Medium 🔮 Secret 0.4% of players

Sit on a bench with another player.

How to unlock: Find one of the wooden benches near the Lodge area. Have a friend also interact with the same bench so you're both seated simultaneously. The achievement pops when two players share the bench.
🎯

Sharp Shooter

Hard 0.3% of players

Hit targets with precision at the shooting range.

How to unlock: Visit the Shooting Range near the Lodge. Complete all target challenges with sufficient accuracy. This same mechanic is used for unlocking Fox and Toad characters — practice your snowball arc here before attempting character unlocks.
🗾

Cross Map

Hard 0.3% of players

Travel across the entire map.

How to unlock: Start from the far right edge of the map and travel all the way to the far left edge (or vice versa) in a single continuous run without teleporting or respawning. Use the flag system strategically and plan a route through all major zones.
🎣

Fisherman

Grind 0.1% of players

Catch many fish.

How to unlock: Catch fish repeatedly over multiple sessions. Community estimates suggest 50–100 fish total. Combine fishing sessions with Hot Chocolate grinding to maintain coin income while working toward this achievement.
🔭

Sniper Training

Hard 0.1% of players

Complete all shooting range challenges.

How to unlock: Complete every target challenge at the Shooting Range. There appear to be multiple rounds of increasing difficulty. This is the hardest aiming-based challenge in the game — practice snowball physics extensively before attempting.
📈

Training Arc

Grind 0.1% of players

Grind a specific skill to maximum.

How to unlock: Dedicate session time to a single activity (fishing, sledding tricks, or shooting) and max out its associated progress bar or counter. Exact requirements are not publicly disclosed — community suggests 8–10 hours of focused play on one activity.
☕

Barista

Hard 🔮 Secret 0.1% of players

Make hot chocolate.

How to unlock: Interact with the hot chocolate bar inside the Lodge from the *server side* of the counter. Most players only interact as customers — the Barista achievement requires finding the interact prompt on the staff side of the bar.
🍵

Drinking Game

Medium 0.1% of players

Play the hot chocolate reaction game.

How to unlock: Stand in front of the Hot Chocolate station outside the Lodge bar and interact with it. A direction-based reaction mini-game will start. Press the correct arrow keys in time to complete a round.
😱

Falling Game

Hard 🔮 Secret 0.1% of players

Fall a great distance.

How to unlock: Climb to the very summit of the mountain, then walk off the steepest cliff edge (not the main slope — find a sheer drop). The exact height required is roughly the full summit-to-base vertical distance in freefall without a sled.
🏅

Professional Drinker

Grind 0.1% of players

Complete the hot chocolate game many times.

How to unlock: Replay the Hot Chocolate reaction mini-game 10–20 times (community estimate). This is also the fastest coin-farming method in the game, so you'll naturally progress toward this achievement while grinding coins.
👑

Sled Master

Legendary 0.1% of players

Master all sledding mechanics.

How to unlock: The ultimate achievement. Requires completing every sledding-related challenge: hit 100 m/s (Speed Demon), perform the biggest jump (Prepare for Takeoff), collide at high speed (Fireball), cross the entire map (Cross Map), and achieve consistent trick combos across multiple runs. No single trigger — it appears to require accumulating a full mastery score across all sled activities.

👑 Sled Master — The Ultimate Achievement

👑

Sled Master

Legendary 0.1% global unlock rate

Sled Master is the prestige achievement of Sledding Game — the final milestone that proves complete mastery of the mountain. With only a 0.1% global unlock rate, fewer than 1 in 1,000 players have earned it.

Requirements (Community Research):

  • 💨 Speed Demon: Hit 100 m/s on a single run.
  • 🚀 Prepare for Takeoff: Launch from the big jump.
  • 🔥 Fireball: Hit another player while moving very fast.
  • 🗺️ Cross Map: Travel from one side of the mountain to the other in one continuous journey.
  • 🎣 Fishing Game / Fisherman: Catch enough fish to hit the hidden grind threshold.
  • ☕ Professional Drinker / Drinking Game: Repeat the Hot Chocolate reaction game many times.

In practice, most players should focus on the achievement chain in this order: Speed Demon → Prepare for Takeoff → Error 404 → Fishing Game → Professional Drinker → Sled Master. That route naturally teaches you the game while moving toward 100% completion.

Helpful if you are working through character unlocks and related achievements.

❓ Achievements FAQ

How many achievements does Sledding Game have? +
Sledding Game has 37 Steam achievements (and 37 Xbox achievements worth 1,000 Gamerscore total). They range from trivially easy (sled once) to extremely rare (Sled Master, 0.1% unlock rate).
What is the rarest achievement in Sledding Game? +
Multiple achievements share the lowest unlock rate of approximately 0.1%, including Sled Master, Barista, Fisherman, Professional Drinker, Training Arc, Sniper Training, and Falling Game. Sled Master is considered the true 100% completion achievement.
