Sledding Game — All Secrets & Hidden Items

Sledding Game hides 5 collectible items, a secret map zone, and several hidden chat commands across its mountain. This guide documents every known secret as of Early Access April 2026 — with exact locations and step-by-step directions.

🎩 All Hidden Collectible Items

Each collectible is a wearable item that cannot be purchased in the Lodge shop. Collecting all 5 is required for the Fashion Star achievement. They respawn on server reset so all players can collect them.

🪖

#1 — Hard Hat

📍 Mid-mountain cave

Directions: On the main slope, look for a cave entrance on the left side around mid-mountain. Inside the cave there is a campfire. The Hard Hat collectible sits near the campfire — interact with it to collect.
🍎

#2 — Apple

📍 Shooting range, on a target

Directions: Go to the Shooting Range near the Lodge. Instead of throwing snowballs, look closely at the targets themselves. One of the targets has an Apple resting on top of it (William Tell style). Interact with the target post or simply walk up and collect it.
🪴

#3 — Flower Pot

📍 Behind the cosmetics/beauty shop inside the Lodge

Directions: Enter the Lodge and find the beauty/cosmetics shop area. Walk behind the shop counter — not the customer side, but the staff-side backroom area. The Flower Pot collectible is hidden on a shelf or surface behind the counter.
🪳

#4 — Cockroach

📍 Under the stone arch bridge, on a small step near the Crown Statue

Directions: Find the Crown Statue on the mountain (a decorative statue shaped like a crown, located on the main downhill path). Sled or walk past it, then look for the stone arch bridge downhill. Under the bridge on the left side there is a small stone ledge or step. The Cockroach sits on this step.

💬 Hidden Chat Commands

Open in-game chat by pressing Enter, type the command exactly as shown, then press Enter again to execute.

/secret

Adds the Golden Tie item to your inventory.

/sit

Makes your character sit on the ground. Unlocks Sitting Game achievement.

/walk

Activates walking mode without a sled. Unlocks Walking Game achievement.

⚠️ Note: Only these three commands are publicly confirmed as of May 2026. Additional commands may exist — the community actively investigates on r/SleddingGame and the official Discord. This page will be updated as new commands are discovered.

🗺️ Hidden Map Areas

🗺️

Map Edge / Error 404 Zone

📍 Left side of map, beyond long chairlift terminal

How to reach it: Take the long left-side chairlift (not the main central one) all the way to its terminal at the top. Once you exit the terminal station, keep running left. There is no visible boundary marker — just keep going. After some distance you will cross the map boundary and the Yeti will appear to kick you back. The Error 404 achievement unlocks here.

🔑 Secrets Hunting Tips

🐢Take your time off the sled

Most collectibles are on foot. Sled past and you will miss them. Slow down and explore on foot especially in cave areas and under bridges.

👀Look for interactable objects

Collectibles glow subtly or have a visual indicator. Approach any unusual object and look for an interact prompt.

🤝Explore with friends

Having multiple people explore different areas simultaneously is the fastest way to discover new secrets. Share findings in proximity chat.

📖Check the Lodge interior thoroughly

The Lodge has multiple rooms including the shop backroom area where Flower Pot is hidden. Many players never leave the main floor.

🔁Revisit areas after updates

New secrets may be added in future Early Access updates. The developer Max has confirmed more content is coming.

💬Try random chat commands

The /secret, /sit, and /walk commands were discovered by players experimenting. Try other slash commands — you might find new ones.

❓ Secrets & Hidden Items FAQ

What are all the chat commands in Sledding Game? +
Known commands: /secret (grants Golden Tie item), /sit (sit on the ground, unlocks Sitting Game achievement), /walk (walk without a sled, unlocks Walking Game achievement). Additional commands may be added in future updates.
What are all the secret items in Sledding Game? +
There are at least 5 known secret items: Hard Hat (mid-mountain cave), Apple (Shooting Range target), Flower Pot (behind Lodge cosmetics shop), Cockroach (under stone arch bridge), and Golden Tie (type /secret in chat).
