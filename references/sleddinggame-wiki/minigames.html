Hot Chocolate Reaction Game

📍 Outside the Lodge bar entrance 👥 Solo 💰 1,000–2,000 per round

A fast-paced directional reaction game. Arrows flash on screen and you must press the matching direction key before the timer runs out. The best solo coin farm in Sledding Game.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Stand at the Hot Chocolate station outside the Lodge bar.
  2. 2 Interact to start the game.
  3. 3 Watch for directional arrows (↑ ↓ ← →) appearing on screen.
  4. 4 Press the matching key (W/A/S/D or arrow keys) in time.
  5. 5 Successfully clearing all arrows in a round rewards coins.
  6. 6 Replay immediately — no cooldown.

💡 Tips:

  • →Arrow keys tend to be more intuitive than WASD for this mini-game.
  • →The sequence speed increases slightly each round — focus and don't rush.
  • →This is the recommended starting farm before you can afford a Fishing Rod.

🏆 Linked: Drinking Game / Professional Drinker / Chicken Dinner

Fishing

📍 Multiple spots on the mountain, best at summit 👥 Solo or Co-op 💰 500–30,000+ per catch depending on fish type

A relaxing fishing mini-game with 20 catchable fish species. Requires a Fishing Rod (10,000 coins). The summit spot offers the rarest and most valuable fish, including the legendary Great White Shark.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Buy a Fishing Rod from the Lodge shop (10,000 coins).
  2. 2 Walk to any fishing spot (blue shimmer indicates a valid fishing location).
  3. 3 Interact to cast your line.
  4. 4 Wait for the bite indicator to appear and activate.
  5. 5 Interact again to reel in.
  6. 6 Check your catch — rare fish are worth significantly more coins.

💡 Tips:

  • →Summit fishing spot has the highest rare fish spawn rate.
  • →Fishing from the summit also has a chance to trigger the secret Fishing Character unlock.
  • →Pair fishing sessions with chatting — proximity voice chat makes it a great social activity.
  • →Catch and keep Great White Sharks for The BIG one achievement.

🏆 Linked: Fishing Game / Fisherman / The BIG one

Darts

📍 Inside the Lodge 👥 Solo or vs. other players 💰 Coin reward for high scores / wins

Classic darts mini-game inside the Lodge. Aim for the bullseye and outperform other players for coin rewards. Improves your projectile aiming skill, which also helps with character unlock challenges.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Enter the Lodge and find the Darts board.
  2. 2 Interact to join a game.
  3. 3 Use mouse aim to throw darts at the board.
  4. 4 Score as close to bullseye as possible each throw.
  5. 5 Player with highest score wins the coin pot.

💡 Tips:

  • →Darts practice directly translates to better snowball throwing for Fox and Toad character unlocks.
  • →Challenge other Lodge players for a more competitive atmosphere.

🏆 Linked: Chicken Dinner

🥌

Curling

📍 Inside the Lodge 👥 Multiplayer (2+ required) 💰 Coin reward for winners

A winter curling mini-game that requires at least two players. Slide your stone as close to the target circle center as possible. A great social activity for groups.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Find the Curling lane inside the Lodge.
  2. 2 Wait for (or invite) another player to join.
  3. 3 Take turns sliding curling stones toward the target circle.
  4. 4 Closest stone to center at end of round wins.

💡 Tips:

  • →Curling is great for friend groups — set up a mini-tournament.
  • →The physics are more forgiving than real curling — focus on power rather than precision.

🏆 Linked: Chicken Dinner / Just the Two of Us

🌽

Cornhole

📍 Lodge outdoor area 👥 Solo or vs. other players 💰 Coin reward for wins

Toss beanbags into the cornhole board. A relaxed outdoor activity near the Lodge. Light on strategy but fun for passing time between sled runs.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Find the Cornhole boards in the Lodge outdoor area.
  2. 2 Take turns tossing bags — aim for the hole (3 points) or land on the board (1 point).
  3. 3 First to 21 points wins.

💡 Tips:

  • →Underhand tosses with medium power tend to be most accurate.

🏆 Linked: Chicken Dinner

🔥

S'mores / Marshmallow Roasting

📍 Campfire areas (multiple, including cave campfire) 👥 Solo or group 💰 Achievement / social activity

A cozy campfire interaction where you roast marshmallows on a stick. No coins awarded but links to campfire-related achievements and is a beloved social activity.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Find a campfire (one is inside the mid-mountain cave, others are scattered around).
  2. 2 Interact with the campfire to begin roasting.
  3. 3 Hold the interact key to roast — don't let it burn.

💡 Tips:

  • →The cave campfire is also the location of the Hard Hat collectible — kill two birds with one stone.
  • →Roasting with other players is a charming social moment — use proximity chat to chat while you roast.
❄️

Snowball Fight

📍 Outdoor slopes and lodge area 👥 2+ players 💰 Achievements

Throw snowballs at other players anywhere on the mountain. No formal arena — it's fully impromptu PvP. Unlocks Choosing Violence achievement and is the training ground for character unlock challenges.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Equip snowballs from your toolbar.
  2. 2 Aim and throw at another player.
  3. 3 Lead moving targets slightly ahead of their direction.
  4. 4 Snowballs arc — aim slightly high for distance shots.

💡 Tips:

  • →Snowball physics practice here directly helps with Toad/Fox character unlock challenges.
  • →Snowballs move faster downhill — use the slope to your advantage.

🏆 Linked: Choosing Violence / Sharp Shooter

⛄

Snowman Building

📍 Outdoor snow areas near Lodge 👥 Solo or co-op

Find the snowman building area and roll snowballs to create a three-tier snowman. A wholesome Sledding Game activity that links to the 'Do you want to' achievement.

How to Play:

  1. 1 Find a snow pile or snowman-designated area near the Lodge.
  2. 2 Interact to begin rolling a snowball.
  3. 3 Roll three sizes (small, medium, large).
  4. 4 Stack them to complete your snowman.

💡 Tips:

  • →Build snowmen with friends nearby to get extra reactions via proximity chat.
