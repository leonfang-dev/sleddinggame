🏔️ What is Sledding Game?

Sledding Game is a cozy online multiplayer experience developed by Max (The Sledding Corporation). Launched into Early Access on April 30, 2026, the game dropped simultaneously as a Day-One Xbox Game Pass title — making it instantly accessible to millions of subscribers worldwide.

The premise is deceptively simple: sled down a snowy mountain with up to 50 players, hang out at the Lodge, play mini-games, fish at the summit, and discover hidden secrets scattered across the map. The game's proximity voice chat system means you can only hear players near you — making every collision, race, and Lodge conversation feel wonderfully intimate and chaotic.

The demo alone drew 80,000 Steam wishlists in its first week — far above what the team expected. VICE described it as "the most adorable little game to exist." With a current 97% Very Positive rating on Steam, Sledding Game is one of 2026's most wholesome breakout indie hits.

👨‍💻 About the creator

Sledding Game was created by Max, publishing under the name The Sledding Corporation. The game took off after its free Demo launch on September 25, 2025 — it picked up 80,000 Steam wishlists in the first week alone, far beyond what the small team expected.

The official @sledding_game Instagram grew to over 533,000 followers, with the Demo showcase video accumulating 2.3 million views. The community Discord at discord.gg/sled quickly became one of the fastest-growing indie game communities of 2025–2026.

The full Early Access launch on April 30, 2026 brought 12+ characters, 37 achievements, 20+ sled types, 8 mini-games, and a Day-One Xbox Game Pass release. Max has committed to a full release within 12 months of Early Access, with the community's feedback actively shaping what comes next.

❓ Sledding Game FAQ

Is Sledding Game on Xbox Game Pass? +
Yes! Sledding Game launched as a Day-One release on Xbox Game Pass on April 30, 2026. It's available for Ultimate and PC Game Pass subscribers on Xbox Series X/S, Xbox Cloud Gaming, and PC.
Who made Sledding Game? +
Sledding Game was created by Max, publishing under the name The Sledding Corporation. After the Demo arrived in September 2025, it picked up about 80,000 Steam wishlists in its first week — much more than most small teams expect from a first showing.
When was Sledding Game released? +
The playable Demo launched on September 25, 2025. The Early Access full release launched on April 30, 2026 on Steam and Xbox Game Pass Day One.
Is Sledding Game multiplayer? +
Yes! Sledding Game is primarily a multiplayer online game supporting up to 50 players per server. It features proximity voice chat, so you can only hear players near your character. Private servers for friend groups are also supported.
What platforms is Sledding Game on? +
Sledding Game is available on PC via Steam, and on Xbox Series X/S and PC via Xbox Game Pass. There is no mobile, PlayStation, or Nintendo Switch version announced as of May 2026.
Is there a free demo for Sledding Game? +
Yes. A free Sledding Game Demo is available on Steam. It offers a taste of the core sledding and Lodge mechanics, though some content is exclusive to the full game.
